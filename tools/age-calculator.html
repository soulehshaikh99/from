What Exactly is an Age Calculator?

If you've ever had questions like:

  • "How old am I today, tomorrow, or 10 years into the future?"
  • "How many days have I walked on this blue planet?"
  • "How many days until my next birthday?"
  • "How old is my favourite clothing brand?"
  • "What's the age of my favourite celebrity?"
  • "The number of years your company has been in operation?"

...then your simple answer is using an Age Calculator.

You could use your fingers to count or scribble on your mind board, but when you need precise and instant results, this Free Age Calculator is your go-to. It uses numerical data like a start date and end date to calculate the age accurately, every time.

No, it won't change its answer no matter how many times you ask. Don't believe us? Let's try it together!

How to Use the Age Calculator?

Our Age Calculator comes with three modes:

  1. Calculate age by Birth Date
  2. Calculate age by Birth Year
  3. Calculate age by Birth Date and Time

Birth Date Mode

This mode is the simplest and most common way to calculate age. You just need to enter your birth date and the date you want to calculate age for. The calculator will then give you the age in years, months, days, hours, minutes, and seconds.

Birth Year Mode

This mode is useful if you know your birth year and want to calculate age for a specific year. You just need to enter your birth year and the year you want to calculate age for. The calculator will then give you the age in years, months, days, hours, minutes, and seconds.

Birth Date and Time Mode

This mode is useful if you know your birth date and time and want to calculate age for a specific date and time. You just need to enter your birth date and time and the date and time you want to calculate age for. The calculator will then give you the age in years, months, days, hours, minutes, and seconds.
